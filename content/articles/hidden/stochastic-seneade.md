# Stochastic Senerade

Probabilistic songs...the future of music?

## Background

I'm in the car, thinking to myself "music has felt samey for as long as I can remember". (At least for me) it feels like music changed more frequently in the decades before I was born (2005). Naturally, I wonder what caused this seeming difference in my mind:

1. Maybe it was the introduction of new types of electronic was to create or play music
2. Okay, so what could be the next thing like that: revolutionising music
3. My quantum loving brain immediately goes there
    1. So quantum computers probably aren't going to revolutionise music
    2. BUT what about randomness?

What if notes were pobabilistic wrt pitch? That you could introduce pitch distributions into your music.

Whilst this is unlikely to be popular in the casual music scene where songs that are catchy stay with you, what about concerts? Maybe far in the future it has a place there.

Either way, let's make it!
